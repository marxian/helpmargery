Meet Margery.

Margery looks after the Brooke Village Hall - she used to take bookings on paper and sometimes forgot bookings and double booked people - now she uses Everyday Spaces and has a widget embedded in her website. Coupled with the tablet her grandchildren bought her for Christmas - the admin system calendar and email alerts allow her to easily manage bookings so she no longer ends up having to suggest they split the hall between 2 bookings!

Meet Roy.

Roy owns a nightclub in Norwich - whilst it's generally booked out most weekends it is empty the rest of the time - and Roy simply doesn't have time to deal with constant enquiries. With our system he is able to let the system do all the hard work. Roy has now been able to make a steady stream of extra income- simply by filling out the details of his venue once.
